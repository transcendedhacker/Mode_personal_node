# Library Comparison

## comprehensive_portrait.json

**Primary Use Cases**: Professional photography, studio portraits, fashion work, artistic compositions

**Characteristic Style**: Polished, posed, intentional, directed photography

**Example Selections**:
- pose: "Over Shoulder" (modeling pose)
- location: "Industrial Loft"
- mood: "Dramatic Noir"
- outfit: "Silk Slip Dress"

**Output Characteristics**: Magazine photography, portfolio work, deliberate artistic direction

---

## everyday_casual.json

**Primary Use Cases**: Candid moments, daily activities, realistic scenarios, documentary-style imagery

**Characteristic Style**: Natural, unposed, authentic, observational photography

**Example Selections**:
- pose: "Looking At Phone" (everyday action)
- location: "Coffee Shop"
- mood: "Relaxed"
- outfit: "Jeans T-Shirt"

**Output Characteristics**: Snapshot aesthetic, social media casual, documentary realism

---

## Key Differences

### Poses

**comprehensive_portrait:**
- "Over Shoulder" - directed modeling pose
- "Weight On Hip" - studio stance
- "Kneeling Upright" - compositional pose

**everyday_casual:**
- "Scrolling Phone" - contemporary activity
- "Tying Shoes" - mundane moment
- "Waiting In Line" - observational behavior

### Locations

**comprehensive_portrait:**
- "Industrial Loft" - studio setting
- "Rooftop Terrace" - scenic backdrop
- "Neon Alley" - cinematic environment

**everyday_casual:**
- "Grocery Store" - commercial space
- "Spa" - service location
- "Mall Food Court" - public setting

### Outfits

**comprehensive_portrait:**
- "Silk Slip Dress" - fashion garment
- "Gold Sequin Gown" - formal wear
- "Latex Mini" - editorial piece

**everyday_casual:**
- "Jeans T-Shirt" - casual wear
- "Hoodie Leggings" - comfort clothing
- "Workout Clothes" - athletic wear

### Moods

**comprehensive_portrait:**
- "Dramatic Noir"
- "Golden Hour Romance"
- "Cyberpunk Nightlife"

**everyday_casual:**
- "Relaxed"
- "Tired"
- "Bored"

---

## Selection Guidelines

**comprehensive_portrait**: Professional photography with directed composition

**everyday_casual**: Observational photography capturing everyday moments

---

## Output Examples

### comprehensive_portrait example:
```
A young woman 23-27 years old with auburn hair, wearing black velvet mini dress,
(turning back to camera, looking over shoulder:1.2), (dramatic noir:1.2), 
in industrial loft, BREAK, (saturated neon pink lighting:1.3)...
```
Output style: Magazine editorial photography

### everyday_casual example:
```
A young woman 23-27 years old with brown hair in a ponytail, wearing jeans and a t-shirt with sneakers,
scrolling on her phone, relaxed casual mood, in a coffee shop during afternoon, 
bright natural daylight, natural daylight illumination, eye-level shot, 
medium shot framing, natural realistic colors, photorealistic, natural lighting, everyday moment
```
Output style: Contemporary documentary photography

Neither library is superior - they serve different compositional objectives.

Directed photography vs observational photography.

Select library based on intended output style.
